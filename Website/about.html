About Us

We are a brand new development company based in Austin, Texas.

We're four students who are excited about high quality development and decided to do something about it. We take things that we are passionate about, and do something about it. By creating high quality products we aim to make the world a better place.

We make cool things.

We created our entire first project, RideSafe, all at HackDFW 2015 with the mission to give the ridesharing community a stronger peace of mind. Our next project, Devscrow, was created for the development community and graphic design community. Everything we do is awesome.

We know what we're doing.

Our lead developer has two successful hackathons under his belt. Our lead project manager has been managing his whole life. Our lead technology officer has has his own IT company since he was in high school. This should all speak for itself.

We know how to work together.

Three members of MoDev Systems have been living together literally their entire life. The fourth has spent every day for nearly a year with them. These kinds of relationships don't just form themselves - they're made over time.

We don't quit.

When we're given a challenge, we take it on, and (literally) won't sleep until it's done. During HackDFW 2015, we worked for 31 hours straight to make RideSafe happen in time. We do our work, and we do it well.
